# GARIBALDI MARKET ORACLE V16 — Explainable AI

V16 adds a deterministic evidence ledger to every Oracle decision. It does not ask a language model to invent a reason. It translates the exact outputs of the Quant Standard, Market Memory, Global Intelligence, Opportunity Radar, Scenario Engine and Capital Allocator into:

- weighted engine votes and consensus;
- strongest supporting drivers and material conflicts;
- a step-by-step decision path;
- objective thesis-invalidation conditions;
- full position-size multiplier attribution;
- confidence-quality labels based on history, uncertainty and agreement.

The worker, stored opportunity payload and dashboard all use the same report.
