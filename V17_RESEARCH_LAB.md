# V17 Institutional Research Lab

V17 adds a deterministic institutional-style research layer to every opportunity. It combines technical evidence, available fundamentals, catalysts, relative value, scenario risk, portfolio fit, Market Memory, and explainability into a structured report.

The engine never invents unavailable fundamentals. Missing company, token, or network data is explicitly listed as a data gap and scored neutrally until a provider supplies it.

Each report includes an investment thesis, research rating, five component scores, bull and bear cases, catalysts, principal risks, confirmation conditions, thesis invalidation rules, and evidence quality.
